About us

My Clever Kitchen was founded in 2018 by Cornelia Johansson, Lovisa Linder Lodén and Zhiyin Li as a response to what they felt other recipe websites were lacking - the option to decide multiple ingredients yourself. To look up recipes is common, for some an everyday thing. But it can be a hassle finding a recipe you like just to realize you’re missing half the things that you need when you’re standing in your kitchen ready to get going. My Clever Kitchen instead asks the question “what do you already have at home?” and gives suggestions that fit whatever the answer to that may be. In addition to that, Clever Kitchen offers a set of additional filtering options to make your search as personalized as possible, giving the option sort on holidays, allergies, dietary restrictions, type of meal etc. And with a library of 5000 recipes, you can be sure to find something you like.

Our vision

    Reduce food waste through cooking with ingredients you already have and otherwise might have forgotten about
    Encourage people to create their own home-cooked meals
    Make finding recipes as smooth and personalized of a journey as possible
